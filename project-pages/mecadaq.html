From March 2024 to August 2024, I worked as a Data Analyst on a 6-month contract at MECADAQ GROUP, a precision engineering company based in Tarnos, Nouvelle-Aquitaine, France. This experience allowed me to strengthen my skills in data management, data integration, and dashboard creation within an industrial context with international dimensions. I also had the opportunity to manage an intern for 4 months.

Database management and integration using Talend
Design and development of dashboards and KPI monitoring tools
Participation in the implementation of a centralised Data Warehouse to harmonise data from several international production sites
details
The main goal of this mission was to support the digitalisation and data harmonisation efforts of the group, which operates across multiple sites in Europe and North America. I contributed to the creation of a unified Data Warehouse, aimed at centralising and standardising production, quality, and logistics data from different subsidiaries. Using Talend, I developed and maintained ETL workflows to clean, transform, and load data into the warehouse.
In parallel, I collaborated with the operations and quality teams to define relevant Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and develop interactive dashboards for internal use. These dashboards helped monitor production efficiency, reduce delays, and identify quality deviations in real time
This experience provided me with hands-on practice in industrial data integration, BI tools, and cross-functional collaboration. It also gave me insights into the challenges of working with heterogeneous data sources in a manufacturing environment.
